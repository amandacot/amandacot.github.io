About Me

Hi! My name is Amanda. I’m in eleventh grade and I have loved computers ever since I was little. I started learning how to code about 5 years ago. I did a summer program with Alexa Cafe, an all-girls coding camp within ID tech. Ever since then, I haven’t stopped coding. I made this website to help others use technology, and I got the idea from my own family. They refer to me as the “technology help-desk”, and I’m used to going over for a holiday and being asked by various people to help fix their phones or computers. So, when the infamous COVID-19 lockdown began, I was getting even more questions from my relatives on technological issues. The goal of my website is to provide clear, easy steps to follow in order to help people use technology without being overwhelmed. I love coding so much and want to use my skills to help others (:
