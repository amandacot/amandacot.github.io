Irish Museum of Modern Art - Shopify Components & UX Work

Information Systems & UX Intern – Summer 2025, Dublin, Ireland

At the Irish Museum of Modern Art (IMMA), I worked at the intersection of art, technology, and visitor experience. My internship focused on customizing IMMA’s Shopify storefront, evaluating the Smartify app in-gallery, and designing cybersecurity awareness campaigns for staff.

What I Did

  • Created and customized Shopify components aligned with IMMA’s visual identity and accessibility needs.
  • Evaluated the Smartify app in-gallery, observing visitors and documenting UX and accessibility issues.
  • Designed phishing awareness campaigns and cybersecurity training content for IMMA staff.

Shopify Storefront Components

I worked on IMMA’s online shop by customizing Shopify templates and sections to better reflect the museum’s brand, support exhibitions, and improve the browsing experience for visitors purchasing books, prints, and gifts.
