Universal Music Group – Artist Storefront Development

Global Technology, D2C Engineering – Summer 2024

During my internship at Universal Music Group (Fame House), I worked on direct-to-consumer artist storefronts powered by Shopify. My work focused on theme customization, Liquid development, UX improvements, and making sure fans had a smooth experience buying music and merch across desktop and mobile.

What I Did

  • Migrated and aligned Shopify 1.0 themes with Shopify 2.0 structures while maintaining artist branding.
  • Customized reusable sections and templates using Liquid, HTML, and CSS.
  • Debugged layout issues, metafields, and theme settings across multiple storefronts.
  • Improved mobile layouts and addressed accessibility considerations.
  • Implemented designs based on Figma comps from the Creative Team.
  • Collaborated with engineers and eCommerce teams to QA storefronts before releases and campaigns.
